About Us

Discover Our Restaurant Story

Cuisine is a family-owned restaurant since 1999. Back in 1999, we decided to quit our day jobs and put all of our money and time into opening this food establishment. Since Y2K was right around the corner, we wanted to make sure that we lived our lives with no regrets just in case the world came to it's end. Our italian upbringing provided us with love and precision when it comes to cooking and we wanted to share that with the local community.

We look forward to providing you with nutritious and delicious meals. From our family, to yours.
